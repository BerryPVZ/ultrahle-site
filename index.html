What UltraHLE is aiming for

A cleaner app picker, stronger game-specific defaults, and less terminal pain. The site is built to show exactly what runs, not to overpromise.

Roadmap

UltraHLE is moving toward boring, reliable compatibility: less command-line fighting, cleaner defaults, and more old apps that can be launched straight from the picker.

  1. Now Lock down the current working roster

    Keep Captain Tomato, Fuzz Friend, HairyBlox, Minion Jump, the Potato games, TomatoZombie, and PvZ Great Wall slots documented with the exact options they need.

  2. Next Mr. Goo and Happy Goat Maybe

    Yeah-ish.

  3. Next Fully Fix PvZ Games

    PvZ2, PvZ HD, PvZ GWE, PVZ EDU, PVZ PA, those are planned.

  4. Later OpenGL ES 2+ support path

    Build out the GLES2 compatibility layer so newer early-iOS games have a real path forward instead of hard-stopping at graphics setup.

  5. Later Make the app picker feel more iOS-like

    Better icons, nicer app details, search, favorites, cleaner errors, and fewer crashes when assets or metadata are weird.
